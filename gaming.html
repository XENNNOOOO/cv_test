Death Stranding

Description:
Death Stranding is a 2019 action-adventure video game developed by Kojima Productions and directed by Hideo Kojima. Set in a post-apocalyptic world, the game centers around a protagonist named Sam Porter Bridges, played by Norman Reedus, who is tasked with delivering packages across a fractured, dangerous landscape. The game explores themes of isolation, connection, and survival in a world where mysterious creatures, known as "BTs" (Beached Things), and a phenomenon called "Death Stranding" have disrupted society.

The story revolves around Sam's journey to reconnect various isolated communities in a fractured America, using a system of communication and transportation to rebuild the country. As players, you must navigate treacherous terrain, avoid hostile entities, and manage resources to make successful deliveries. The gameplay emphasizes cooperation, where players can leave helpful items or structures for others in the online world.
